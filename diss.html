"The Warning" (Diss towards Mariah Carey)

"The Warning" was released in 2009 as a response to Mariah Carey's song "Obsessed," which was rumored to be about Eminem. In the diss track, Eminem accuses Mariah of lying about their alleged relationship and warns her not to continue spreading false rumors. The track includes explicit language and personal attacks on Mariah Carey and her then-husband Nick Cannon.

"Nail in the Coffin" (Diss towards Benzino)

Eminem and rapper Benzino engaged in a long-standing feud, with both artists releasing multiple diss tracks aimed at each other. "Nail in the Coffin" was Eminem's response to Benzino's disses, particularly "Pull Your Skirt Up." In this track, Eminem delivers scathing insults and dismisses Benzino's credibility in the hip-hop community.

"Quitter" (Diss towards Everlast)

Eminem and Everlast, known for his work with the group House of Pain, had a brief feud in the early 2000s. "Quitter" was Eminem's response to Everlast's track "Whitey's Revenge." In the diss, Eminem accuses Everlast of being a hypocrite and disses his musical abilities.

"Killshot" (Diss towards Machine Gun Kelly)

"Killshot" was released by Eminem in 2018 as a response to Machine Gun Kelly's "Rap Devil." In the diss track, Eminem takes shots at MGK's career and mocks his appearance and music. Eminem also addresses the tweet MGK made about his daughter, Hailie, which sparked the initial feud between the two artists. "Killshot" quickly gained massive attention and intensified the public feud between Eminem and MGK.
