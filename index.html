Eminem, whose real name is Marshall Bruce Mathers III, is an American rapper, songwriter, record producer, and actor.

He was born on October 17, 1972.

Eminem is widely regarded as one of the greatest and most influential rappers in the history of hip-hop.

His lyrics often delve into personal and controversial topics, touching on his struggles with poverty, family issues, addiction, and his experiences with fame.

Eminem's raw and emotionally charged style, along with his exceptional wordplay and storytelling abilities, make him unique

Throughout his career, Eminem has sold millions of records, becoming one of the best-selling artists of all time.

Eminem's impact on hip-hop culture and music as a whole is significant, and he continues to be a prominent figure in the music industry.
